## 01. Introduction

### 1.1 About Chimadev

Chimadev is a creative technology studio specializing in web development, workflow automation, AI-powered software, and internal tools for modern teams. The brand blends expressive creativity with futuristic system design, positioning Chimadev as a builder of intelligent, human-centered digital experiences.

### 1.2 Brand Philosophy

Chimadev stands at the intersection of imagination and engineering. The brand exists to transform ideas into scalable systems — systems that feel intuitive, powerful, and future-ready.

### 1.3 Brand Promise

To deliver digital solutions that are as creative as they are intelligent, as human as they are technical, and as scalable as they are beautiful.

## 04. Logo System

### 4.1 Logo Concept

The Chimadev logo is a fusion of creativity and structure. It combines expressive energy with modular geometry, reflecting the brand's dual nature: imaginative and engineered.

### 4.2 Primary Wordmark

- Futuristic geometric letterforms
- Rounded edges for warmth
- Subtle modular cuts in letters such as C, A, and V

### 4.3 Secondary Mark

**C•[]**

- **C** — Chimadev
- **•** — creativity, spark, ignition
- **[]** — modular systems, structure, code

### 4.4 Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the width of the "C[]" symbol around all sides.

### 4.5 Minimum Size

- **Digital:** 24px height
- **Print:** 15mm height

### 4.6 Incorrect Usage

- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not rotate
- Do not apply unapproved colors
- Do not add shadows or outlines
- Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds

## 05. Typography

### 5.1 Primary Typeface — JetBrains Mono

**Used for:**

- Hero headlines
- Section titles
- Body text
- UI labels
- Code snippets

**Tone:** futuristic, clean, technical, high-performance.

### 5.2 Secondary Typeface — None

To maintain a lean, developer-centric aesthetic, Chimadev uses **JetBrains Mono** exclusively for all typographic needs.

### 5.3 Type Scale

| Token    | Size  | Font                     |
| -------- | ----- | ------------------------ |
| H1       | 60px  | JetBrains Mono Bold      |
| H2       | 44px  | JetBrains Mono SemiBold  |
| H3       | 30px  | JetBrains Mono Medium    |
| Body L   | 18px  | JetBrains Mono Regular   |
| Body M   | 16px  | JetBrains Mono Regular   |
| Mono     | 15px  | JetBrains Mono Regular   |
| Micro    | 12px  | JetBrains Mono Medium    |

### 5.5 Line Height

- **Headlines:** 1.1–1.2
- **Body:** 1.5–1.7
